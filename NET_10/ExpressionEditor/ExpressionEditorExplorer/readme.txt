ExpressionEditor samples for WPF
------------------------------------------
Shows the main features of the ExpressionEditor and ExpressionEditorPanel controls.

Shows the following features:
    - Overciew: how to integrate ExpressionEditor with FlexGrid control.
    - DataGrid: how to integrate ExpressionEditor with DataGrid control.
    - Customization: how to customize ExpressionEditor and ExpressionEditorPanel appearance.
