InputExplorer Samples
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Shows main samples of the C1Input control.

Shows the following controls
	- C1Button
	- C1ToggleButton
	- C1TextBox
	- C1ComboBox
	- C1FilePicker
	- C1NumericBox
	- C1ColorPicker
	- C1HyperlinkButton
	- C1DateTimePicker
	- C1DatePicker
	- C1DropDownButton
	- C1SplitButton
