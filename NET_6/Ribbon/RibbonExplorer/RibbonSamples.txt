Toolstrip (ToolStrip.xaml)
=======================
Shows lightweight C1ToolbarStrip control that supports horizontal and vertical
orientation and overflow handling.

Toolbar (Toolbar.xaml)
===========================
Represents C1Toolbar control that supports grouping, separators and different
button types..

Commands (ToolbarCommands.xaml)
================================
Shows C1Toolbar with command support and various size of toolbar buttons.
